About Nimbus

A 360° Intelligent Application Development Company, spearheaded by techno-commercial thought leaders and visionaries, aided by an incredibly talented team, infusing innovation with technology to increase value proposition of "being"

For us, Diversity and Innovation goes hand in hand and we strive to retain our core competencies at all level and at any cost. At Nimbus Systems Software Solutions, we are a staunch believer of

  • Excellence over Mediocrity
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Equal Employment Opportunity
  • Employee First culture

A near perfect mix of work-life balance provides us that extra cushion to accelerate in crunch times to overcome any obstacles, we may collide with. It’s all about TEAM WORK, KNOWLEDGE & APPETITE FOR EXCELLENCE that sets us apart to stay ahead in the game.

Nimbus Systems Software Solutions envisions a future with exponential growth in revenue, using the power of Data Science. With our robust Artificial Intelligence based solutions, we intend to disrupt processes for the ultimate improvement and eliminate human intervention, so that businesses can engage their workforce into more productive work. Our end to end Data Science based solutions has the potential to change the way conventional application works, to commence the advent of a new era called “Automation”.
