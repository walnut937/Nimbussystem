Social Fitness App

This application allows users to track their health goals and progress with little effort, and it can connect to all available smart fitness devices to make it even easier. It has a constant stream of curated fitness videos to help viewers learn healthy habits and exercise routines. Users may also use this app as an exercise and dietary search engine to help them achieve their health objectives by providing a seamless and fluid search experience.

  • An easy-to-use and understand user interface.
  • Managing and maintaining master data of different parameters
  • Absolve unwanted access
  • High security to keep users’ data secured

Bayshore took up the challenge to overcome all the problems listed above and make an application that handles both user-friendliness and diminishes all the problems a regular user faces. We developed an application that takes minimal effort to register and track one’s progress. The application is designed to provide seamless streaming of different exercise videos so as to ease users’ know-how about the exercise.

  • Users can easily register on the system.
  • Users can track their daily progress and diet chart with just the click of a button.
  • Easy to create a workout regime
  • A search engine for exercises that provides a smooth and seamless search experience
