The term Intelligent Automation refers to an advanced form of automation, featuring machines that mimic human actions and possess cognitive abilities, including natural language processing, speech recognition, and computer vision technology. Such intelligent machines decipher a vast amount of structured and unstructured data, analyze, understand, and learn it on the fly, and intelligently automate processes to boost operational efficiency as well as business efficiency. Automation is becoming more and more common in the digital world, and technologies are evolving and adding more human capabilities to machines each day. Automating processes and utilizing AI to increase business efficiency and smart processes are essential in creating exceptional experiences - the ones that are not only personally tailored, but also distinct enough to keep customers coming back. While AI and automation are not new concepts in the technology landscape, advances in digital technology, sensors, and increased computing power have enabled these concepts to spread far and wide in the global business world. Take a moment to see the differences between Intelligent Automation and automation. The process of automation seems to involve the transfer of tasks that humans perform to machines. Nevertheless, in reality, automation is fundamentally transforming the way business processes work. Several different types of automation exist, including fixed automation, programmable automation, robotic process automation, and robotics. Intelligent Automation differs from normal IT automation, given the addition of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence capabilities. In the case of intelligent automation, however, the system will identify the problem and self-correct it without human intervention. As a result of self-learning and the application of intelligence, businesses become more efficient in terms of time and effort and improve customer service.
